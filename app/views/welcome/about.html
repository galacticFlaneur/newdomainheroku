Who we are

ZeroSum allows traders to compete with each other and not the market. We believe the current trading environment is full of rent seeking institutions. What do we mean exactly? Well, for those of us who don't have millions of dollars, trading costs are an astronomically large hidden fee in our returns. By competing with each other and not using traditional brokers, we believe we you can have a better experience with the markets.

We do not provide trading in securities or other market instruments. We create and run competitions designed specifically to identify trading talent and skill. We are creating an environment that places each trader on equal footing and letting the contest determine who is the best trader. You win cash prizes by your relative performance to your peers in our Competitions, not market performance.
